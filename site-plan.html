Web Application for Recipe Management

Purpose

The purpose of this web application is to provide users with a platform to manage and organize their recipes. Users will be able to store their favorite recipes, create custom recipe collections, search for recipes, and share their recipes with others. The application will also provide features for meal planning and generating shopping lists based on selected recipes.

Audience

The target audience for this web application is individuals who enjoy cooking and want a convenient way to organize their recipes. It can be useful for both amateur cooks who want to experiment with different recipes and experienced cooks who want to manage their vast collection of recipes.
